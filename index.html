In a galaxy far, far away, the Sith Lord Darth Therion Vol'Phos rises to power...

With the dark side of the Force, he seeks to conquer the universe and bring it under his rule...

Will you join him in his quest for dominance, or will you stand against the darkness?

The fate of the galaxy is in your hands...

About

Tomax Rich is a seasoned game developer with a passion for creating immersive experiences. This game is built using:

  • JavaScript for game logic
  • HTML and CSS for the front-end

Game Controls

Use WASD to move, mouse to aim and shoot.
